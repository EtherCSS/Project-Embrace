EMBRACE

About Project Embrace

Project Embrace is the official school innovation program of Victoria National High School. It aims to foster a supportive, inclusive, and nurturing learning environment that empowers learners academically, emotionally, and socially. The project unifies the school community—teachers, students, parents, and stakeholders—in building programs that enhance performance, promote well-being, and strengthen community involvement.

Programs

Academic Support

Focused interventions designed to strengthen learner competencies, mastery, and performance.

Well-being & Guidance

Programs that nurture emotional resilience, mental health, and student welfare.

Community Engagement

Partnerships and collaborative activities that connect the school with the wider community.

Leadership & Innovation

Training and activities that develop future-ready, socially responsible school leaders.
